About ACD Pune 2023

Join us October 7th for AWS Community Day Pune 2023 organised by AWS User Group Pune. AWS Community and guest speakers will dive deep into the latest trends, techniques, and best practices for deploying, managing, securing, and scaling your workloads. The day will feature solution demos to provide practical insights. Attendees will walk away with most useful tools, mental models, and resources to innovate, optimise, and scale their applications.

Why Join Us

  • Learn industry trends and intricacies about innovative implementations from AWS experts
  • Harness the power of networking with 1000+ AWS users, DevOps, developers & solutions architects
  • Network with fellow AWS veterans & techies during breaks
  • Take helpful and valuable information back to business
  • Gain visibility to get more publicity for your esteemed organisation
  • Sporting chance for active participants to win AWS goodies
  • Renew, revive & revitalize to exemplify your excitement about the work you do
  • Learn industry trends and intricacies about innovative implementations from AWS experts
